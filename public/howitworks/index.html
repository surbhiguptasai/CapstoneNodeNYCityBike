    • Experience NYC in a whole new way

    NYC Bike is the nation's largest bike share program, with 10,000 bikes and 600 stations across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens and Jersey City. It was designed for quick trips with convenience in mind, and it’s a fun and affordable way to get around town.

    Join

    Become an Annual Member or buy a short-term pass.

    Unlock

    Find an available bike nearby, and get a ride code or use your member key to unlock it.

    Ride

    Take as many short rides as you want while your pass or membership is active.

    Return

    Return your bike to any station, and wait for the green light on the dock to make sure it's locked.

    Rules of the Road

    Ride with the traffic

    Obey traffic signals

    Stay off the sidewalk

    Yield to pedestrians
